Welcome to The Agricultural Promotion and Investment Corporation of Odisha Limited (APICOL)

Established on 01-03-1996, APICOL aims to promote agro-based industries, food processing, and commercial agriculture across Odisha. Our goal is to drive investment in agriculture and allied sectors, offering guidance for project formulation, enterprise development, and project implementation.
